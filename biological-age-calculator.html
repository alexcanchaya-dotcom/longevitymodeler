Methodology & Disclaimer

This calculator implements the PhenoAge algorithm from Levine et al. 2018. PhenoAge uses 9 blood biomarkers plus chronological age to estimate biological aging.

Important: This is for educational purposes only. Results should not replace professional medical advice. Consult healthcare professionals for health decisions.

Biomarker coefficients are based on mortality risk modeling from NHANES III data. Individual results may vary based on lab methods and timing of blood draw.
