Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Longevity Insights Calculator ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website longevitymodeler.com.

By using our website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use our calculator, you voluntarily provide health and lifestyle information including:

  • Age, gender, height, and weight
  • Physical activity levels and exercise habits
  • Dietary and nutritional information
  • Sleep quality and duration
  • Lifestyle habits (smoking, alcohol consumption)
  • Stress levels and mental health indicators
  • Medical history and health conditions

Important: We do NOT store any of the health information you enter into the calculator. All calculations are performed in your browser, and your personal data never leaves your device.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information including:

  • IP address and geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website addresses
  • Date and time of your visit

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

3.1 Google AdSense Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Serve personalized ads based on your interests
  • Show ads based on your previous visits to our website and other websites
  • Measure ad effectiveness and engagement
  • Prevent showing you the same ad repeatedly

Third-Party Ad Vendors: Google and other third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website. These vendors may collect information about your online activities across different websites.

3.2 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • How often users visit our site
  • What pages they visit and in what sequence
  • How long they stay on each page
  • Where they came from before visiting our site

This information is used to improve our website and provide better user experiences.

3.3 Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising: Visit aboutads.info/choices or optout.networkadvertising.org
  • Google Ads Settings: Visit google.com/settings/ads to manage your ad preferences
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent

Note: Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to access certain features.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our longevity calculator service
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: Google AdSense, Google Analytics, and other service providers who help us operate our website

  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or transfer of our assets

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Our website uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

Since we don't store your calculator inputs, there is no personal health data to access, correct, or delete. However, you can manage cookies and tracking as described in Section 3.3.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your calculator data is processed entirely in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

8. Children's Privacy
